Website Evaluations

Good CRAP Design: YouTube

YouTube is a site where you can watch videos created by individual people or big companys.

  • Contrast: YouTube has great contast betweem the navigation and the text and videos on screen.
  • Repetition: YouTube uses repitition, so that the user can easily identify the different items on the page.
  • Alignment: the allignment of the page is great the videos are sligtly off center because of the navigation, but everything feels like it is in place.
  • Proximity: the videos are evenly spaced so tha they dont collide as is the same wit the navigation.

Bad CRAP Design: Atari Best Electronics

This is a site for a business called Atari Best Electronics where they document and sell Atari parts and accessories.

  • Contrast: The contrast on this site is awful I counted 13 different colors on the page and it is mainly text on a plain white background
  • Repetition: There are issues with repitition as there are some centred paragraphs while the rest are allined correctly.
  • Alignment: There seems to be no consideration for the allignmet of the text it is just the default allignment with some centered objects.
  • Proximity: It seems that the proximity of the text to the edge of the screen was not looked at when designing this page making it hard to read.
